Welcome to the neighborhood!

Greater Rockwell Organization (GRO) has been advocating on behalf of our neighbors for over 40 years, and our service boundaries are from Wilson to Lawrence and Western to Virginia (at the river). Our monthly meetings are held the first Tuesday of each month (except January) at the Lincoln Square Presbyterian Church Community Space located at 4635 North Rockwell. Please come by to say "hi" or to share your ideas or concerns! We look forward to meeting you soon.

How are Utility Rates Set and How Do They Impact Me?

November's monthly GRO meeting featured Jeff Ocrutt, formerly the personal policy advisor for Illinois Commerce Commissioner Miguel del Valle. Jeff addressed the group on ComEd's history and explained how the state of Illinois sets the utility's customer billing rates and what the latest fights down in Springfield may mean for customers in the future.
